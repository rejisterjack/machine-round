## Slide 1 — Hook

**Title:** Train for the interviewer that isn't human

- Personal story: AI screening round days before this hackathon
- Candidates prep for humans; real screens are scored by models
- Namaste Machine Round closes that gap

---

## Slide 2 — Problem

**Title:** Prep tools optimize the wrong interview

- Rapport, storytelling, body language ≠ what AI evaluators score
- AI screens reward clarity, structure, specificity, conciseness
- No dedicated product trains for machine evaluators today

---

## Slide 4 — Two-agent architecture (AI fluency)

**Title:** Interviewer ≠ evaluator

| Agent | Role | When |
|---|---|---|
| Interviewer | Adaptive questions in real time | During session |
| Evaluator | Structured scoring over full transcript | After session |

- Separating roles improves quality vs one agent doing both
- Optional RAG grounding from Neon pgvector question bank
